Go through the Race Condition, Starvation concepts.


Deadlock:
    Deadlock is a situation when two threads are waiting for each other and the waiting is never ends.
    Here both threads cant completes their tasks.

Starvation:
    In Starvation, threads are also waiting for each other. But here waiting time is not infinite
    after some interval of time, waiting thread always gets the resources whatever is required to
    execute thread run() method.
